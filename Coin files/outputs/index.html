How Rando Works

The rules are simple. The outcomes are not.

Step I

Buy & Hold

Acquire ≥ 0.25% of supply. Hold for the full draw interval without selling. Any sell — even 1 token — resets your clock to zero. The eyes remember.

Step II

Fees Accumulate

Every trade generates fees. 100% flows to the reward wallet. SOL piles up between draws. The wallet is patient. The wallet is always patient.

Step III

The Draw

First draw fires 20 minutes after launch. Each draw adds 20 minutes to the next interval. Caps at 6 hours. One random eligible holder wins 33% of the pot.

Step IV

The Patience Tax

If the pot drops below 0.5 SOL, draws pause. Fees keep flowing in. When it refills, the next winner collects everything. No warning. No schedule.
